Cold Coffee

⏱ 5 mins 🔥 Easy

What you need

  • Milk
  • Coffee powder
  • Sugar
  • Ice cubes

How to make it

  1. Add all ingredients to a blender
  2. Blend for 30 seconds
  3. Pour into a glass
  4. Serve chilled
