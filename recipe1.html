Maggi

⏱ 10 mins 🔥 Easy

Ingredients

  • Maggi noodles
  • Water
  • Masala
  • Butter (optional)

Steps

  1. Boil water
  2. Add noodles + masala
  3. Cook for 2–3 minutes
  4. Add butter and serve hot
