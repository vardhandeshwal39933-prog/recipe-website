Grilled Sandwich

⏱ 10 mins 🔥 Easy

What you need

  • Bread slices
  • Butter
  • Cheese
  • Vegetables (onion, tomato)

How to make it

  1. Spread butter on bread
  2. Add veggies and cheese
  3. Grill until golden brown
  4. Cut and serve hot
